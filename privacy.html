Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 1, 2026  ·  Questions: legal@sceneone.net

What We Collect

Name, email, uploaded script files, analysis history, scores, revision logs, Creative Choice selections, payment information (processed by Stripe — we never see your card details), and Discovery Dashboard profile data for reader accounts.

Script File Retention

  • Free Tier: 7 days from analysis completion, then permanently deleted
  • Pro ($12/mo): 30 days from analysis completion, then permanently deleted
  • Pro Unlimited ($29/mo): 60 days from analysis completion, then permanently deleted

You may manually delete your scripts at any time. Coverage reports are retained for the life of your account and can be exported.

No Data Sales

SceneOne does not sell your data to third parties. Ever.

No AI Training

Your scripts are not used to train, fine-tune, or improve AI models without your explicit written consent obtained separately.

AI Disclosure

All analysis is AI-generated. This is disclosed at the point of upload, within every report, and throughout these terms — in compliance with applicable and emerging AI disclosure regulations.

Cookies

SceneOne uses cookies strictly for authentication (Supabase session management) and security (Cloudflare). We do not use advertising or tracking cookies. No consent banner is shown because functional/security cookies are strictly necessary under applicable law.

Your Rights

You may request a copy of your data, request deletion of your account, export your reports, or manually delete scripts at any time. Contact legal@sceneone.net.

International Users

Data is processed in the United States (Supabase West US — Oregon). EU/UK users: SceneOne operates under GDPR principles. Contact legal@sceneone.net to exercise rights of access, correction, or deletion.

Third-Party Services

SceneOne uses Supabase (database and auth), Stripe (payments), Anthropic (AI analysis), Cloudflare (CDN and security), and Google Fonts (typography). Each operates under their own privacy policy. No script content is sent to Stripe, Cloudflare, or Google Fonts.

Questions about your data? Email legal@sceneone.net — we respond within 5 business days.
